Fruit Salad Recipe

A simple and refreshing mix of fresh fruits, perfect for breakfast or a light dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey.
  3. Toss gently and serve fresh.
