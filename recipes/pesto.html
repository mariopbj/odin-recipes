Pesto

Pesto sauce is a classic Italian recipe from Genoa, made with fresh basil, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic. Itâ€™s perfect for pasta, sandwiches, and salads, bringing a fresh and aromatic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts (or walnuts)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the basil leaves.
  2. Place basil, pine nuts, and garlic in a food processor and blend.
  3. Add Parmesan cheese and blend again.
  4. With the processor running, slowly add olive oil until creamy.
  5. Season with salt and mix well.
