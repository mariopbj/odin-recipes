Scrambled Eggs

Fluffy scrambled eggs made in just minutes, perfect for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in eggs and stir until softly set.
